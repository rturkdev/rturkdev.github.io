About Us

We are a family-owned business based in Ottawa, Ontario striving to provide delicious quality coffee, convenience and excellent service to workplaces and businesses across the city all while remaining affordable and socially responsible.


What we offer:

Convenience
At Mug Shot Coffee, we are committed to providing you with the most convenient and hassle-free gourmet coffee service around. We take care of everything, so that you don’t have to.

No Cost to You
Best of all, IT’S FREE! That’s right - We provide the machine, the beans and all the top quality coffee products. We also do all of the cleaning, filling and maintenance of the machine. It is truly a win-win proposition. *For more information click here

Customization
Each drink selection can be tailored specifically to your liking! Beverages can be made stronger, weaker, larger, smaller, sweater etc. Furthermore, if you’re not loving a drink, no problem! Beverages can be removed, added or replaced. Don’t like a certain type of espresso bean? That can be all be customized to your preference!

Efficiency
We provide the only coffee system capable of producing high quality traditional Italian espresso without requiring the expertise of a trained barista. Using our ‘bean to cup’ system provides a perfect cup – at the push of a button.
